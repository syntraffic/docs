|ST| Overview

About
-------

|ST| helps your business by improving the uptime of your public Websites and APIs.

* **Real-time monitoring** ensures your team is notified the second an issue is detected.
* **Smart Alerts** notify you only about important information, helping you deal with alert storm gracefully.
* Monitor your website from any **geographical region** of the world.
* **Daily and monthly reports** help you gain valuable insight into your key performance metrics and trends.
* **Public status** pages help you communicate with your customers and handle downtime gracefully.
